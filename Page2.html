Part 1 : The beginning

Perhaps you remember that time when you asked me why I kept saying that you were cute. Because I do. It was right after we watched the movie Clue all together. I went outside trying to catch a glimpse of the red moon you had the chance to take a picture of, but it was obviously too late for that.

Instead, I went for a walk and called you. While introducing you to the street I grew up on, we came across a cat. You had this sweet voice and insisted on seeing him (the cat). Seeing you like this was very touching, it made my heart melt. So I said it, said that you were cute. Confused, you asked me why I kept saying that, because you couldn’t quite get it.

Maybe you forgot about it, but that night, I promised that I will show you, one day. The thing is, that day never came. To be honest, that was very foolish of me. Time doesn’t wait. Still, a promise is a promise, so let me answer your question today.

But first, let’s go back to that night. The one from the 27th to the 28th of August. It was a long & scary night. You weren’t working the next day thankfully, but stayed up all night waiting for the sky to finally get quiet. It was also my first time truly understanding what you being in a country in war meant. So, we stayed talking all night.

You didn’t say a thing about it, but I might have stressed you out with all my questions, and for that I am sorry. But also, it’s true that being with you made me happy. First, because I could make sure you were alright, but also because I got to spend some extra time with you.

And even in such a stressful situation, you were incredibly sweet and patient. You explained everything to me and reassured me when it should’ve been the opposite. That day, I really got to see just how big your heart really is.

As a first, I was getting sleepy. Therefore, I blasted some music onto my headphones trying to stay with you and keep you company until the nightmare is over. And yes, what you are hearing right now is what had been playing on loop that night. I remember it like it was yesterday.

Actually, I listened to this band all night long — and you’re the one who made me discover it. I also really like Tear You Apart. It reminds me of that time you played it on the server. You said it was reminding you of your best friend and you, and that I shouldn’t worry about you two. That was particularly sweet because I didn’t tell you anything, yet you anticipated that I could be jealous and wanted to set things up right away.

That’s one of the things that made me confident over the fact that you were someone to be trusted. And, speaking of jealousy, it reminds me of that one other night. Yes, I know you have it.

I was bored and for some reason, Robin and I started sending idiots kissing gifs to each other. I don’t even get how it happened, but one thing is sure, I clearly minimized the impact this would have on you. I left you hurt and this simple thought is enough to make me sick.

Still, it was unexpected to see you caring so much about this, about « us ». My first thought was to call you, to « repair » things as best as I could. Thankfully, you took the call. As soon as your voice reached my ear, only one wish resided in me. The one to hug you as strong as I could while I whisper to you that I’m all and fully yours.

We ended up spending the whole night on a call. What used to stress me (being alone with you for a little while) became something so natural and heartwarming. Each day, I was learning a bit more about you, your life, your habits.

For the very first time, I was feeling like you were truly inviting me into your intimity and nothing ever touched me more than that. You told me about your parents, your old pets, your insecurities… You opened up to me like never before. It felt like opening a treasure chest. Face to me was something both so precious and so fragile. Something that I shamelessly wanted to keep for myself and protect.

It reminds me of that other sleepless night. Not long before, I asked you to show me your long lost OCs, and you said maybe one day. Well, that night was the moment. One after another, you grabbed your sketchbooks and showed me every single page of them. All of them were full of life, representing years of you that I didn’t get the chance to know.

Not just your artistic evolution was sleeping on the paper, but also your mental one. I saw your heart bleed, I saw you falling in love, I saw you hurt, healing, motivated… I saw another side of you, your history.

To you, this must’ve felt like getting naked. Expose all of your weak points and pray I never turn them against you. So, I can only take this as a big sign of trust. I know only a very few people ever had the chance to see this, and for that I am more than grateful.

To this day, you still can’t imagine just how protective I am towards that child you were. I know they are still scared, but they deserve the love and respect they couldn’t get. Now and forever.
